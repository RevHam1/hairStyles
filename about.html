About Me

Hello my name is Nyway Wright. I’m a licensed hairstylist. I have my own business as a hair stylist. I also sell hair, lashes and custom made wigs. My motto is to give you a look that is upscale, exquisite and contemporary. I cater to both male and female, and also to the young & the young in heart, creating a look just for you.

My hair is 100% unprocessed Virgin hair. I have hair on hand but what I don’t have can be ordered which takes 2-3 Business days.
